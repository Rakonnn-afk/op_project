About One Piece

One Piece is a legendary anime and manga series created by Eiichiro Oda. It tells the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a spirited young pirate who dreams of finding the world's greatest treasure, the One Piece, and becoming the King of the Pirates. Along his journey, Luffy gathers a diverse crew known as the Straw Hat Pirates, each with their own unique dreams and abilities. Together, they explore mysterious islands, battle powerful enemies, and forge unbreakable friendships. First aired in 1999, One Piece has grown into one of the longest-running and most beloved series in history, inspiring fans with its themes of freedom, adventure, and never giving up on your dreams. Welcome to the world of One Piece — a place where the adventure never ends!

Pirate Crew

Straw Hat Pirates

Led by Monkey D. Luffy, the Straw Hat Pirates are a diverse group of individuals united by their dreams and unwavering loyalty to their captain. Their journey began in the East Blue and has taken them across the Grand Line and into the New World. Each member brings unique skills: Zoro's swordsmanship, Nami's navigation, Usopp's marksmanship, Sanji's culinary expertise, Chopper's medical knowledge, Robin's archaeology, Franky's shipwright abilities, Brook's music, and Jinbei's fish-man strength. Together, they aim to find the legendary treasure, One Piece, and make Luffy the Pirate King.​

Red-Haired Pirates

Under the command of "Red-Haired" Shanks, one of the Four Emperors, the Red-Haired Pirates are known for their strength and balance. Shanks, a former member of the legendary Roger Pirates, values peace but won't hesitate to fight when necessary. His crew, including Benn Beckman, Lucky Roux, and Yasopp, is renowned for their combat prowess and camaraderie. They play a significant role in maintaining balance in the New World.

Blackbeard Pirates

Led by Marshall D. Teach, also known as Blackbeard, this crew is infamous for its ruthless pursuit of power. Blackbeard is the only known person to wield two Devil Fruit abilities, making him exceptionally dangerous. His crew comprises formidable individuals, including former Impel Down prisoners and powerful fighters like Shiryu and Laffitte. Their ambition is to dominate the seas and claim the title of Pirate King.​

Big Mom Pirates

The Big Mom Pirates, commanded by Charlotte Linlin (Big Mom), operate as a massive family-based organization. Big Mom’s vision is to create a world where all races live together, but under her authoritarian rule. Her crew is largely composed of her many children, who hold significant power within the ranks. They control Totto Land, an archipelago where sweets and strange life forms exist thanks to Big Mom’s Soul-Soul Fruit powers. Despite the colorful and sweet appearance of their territory, the Big Mom Pirates are ruthless, politically manipulative, and capable of immense violence to maintain their dominance.

Beasts Pirates

The Beast Pirates are a militaristic pirate crew led by the mighty Kaido, one of the Four Emperors. Their organization resembles a massive army, where power is everything. Kaido aims to create a world of chaos and war by building an army of Devil Fruit users, particularly through the use of artificial SMILE fruits. The crew is divided into several ranks, including the elite All-Stars, Headliners, and Gifters. Under Kaido's rule, strength determines one’s worth. Their brutal methods and overwhelming military power made them a dominant force in the New World until their downfall during the Wano Country arc.

Heart Pirates

The Heart Pirates, led by Trafalgar D. Water Law, are a notable crew with a strong reputation for their strategic brilliance and medical expertise. Originally from North Blue, Law and his crew sail in a unique submarine called the Polar Tang, enabling them to operate both above and beneath the sea. Law himself is a skilled doctor and a powerful fighter thanks to the powers of the Op-Op Fruit. The crew often avoids unnecessary battles but are highly capable when cornered. They have strong ties to the Straw Hat Pirates and played key roles during major conflicts such as the Dressrosa and Wano arcs.

Kid Pirates

The Kid Pirates are a fearsome and aggressive pirate crew led by Eustass "Captain" Kid, a notorious member of the Worst Generation. Known for their brutal tactics and thirst for chaos, the crew hails from South Blue and made their mark in the New World through sheer violence and force. Kid himself possesses the power of the Jiki Jiki no Mi (Magnet-Magnet Fruit), which allows him to manipulate metal and create deadly magnetic weapons in battle. The Kid Pirates follow a straightforward philosophy: crush anyone who stands in their way. Despite suffering heavy losses in battles against Yonko crews, including Kaido and Shanks, Kid’s ambition to rise to the top of the pirate world remains unshaken. His right-hand man, Killer, is both a loyal friend and deadly combatant known for his dual scythe-blades and fearsome fighting style. Together, the crew is known for their recklessness, pride, and refusal to back down, even against overwhelming odds.
